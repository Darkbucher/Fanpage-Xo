Secrets of the Singing Voice

"I really felt a sense of despair, wondering if I could never sing again. It was when I couldn't sing that I realized how much I loved singing."

  • Weekend was influenced by her father, who was active in a band, and has been living a musical life since she was a child.
  • During the early years of elementary school, she became familiar with songs from musicals like "Annie," but as a junior high school student, she became obsessed with Avril Lavigne and started singing while playing the guitar.
  • Perhaps influenced by Avril Lavigne, she was eager to create English lyrics from an early age and also experienced living abroad.
  • At the age of 15, he damaged her vocal cords from excessive practice. The only option was six months of silence treatment. After recovery, she acquired her current singing voice.
  • Actually, this singing voice is still not completely healed. If it were to fully heal, this wonderful singing voice would change its form.

Therefore, weekend continues to sing without her vocal cords fully recovering.
